About

EDUCATION

2018: Cineplex Entertainment Film Program, Canadian Film Centre, Toronto, Ontario.
2006: Advanced Television and Film: Graduate Diploma, Sheridan College, Oakville, Ontario.
2004: Bachelor of Fine Arts, Alberta University for The Arts, Calgary, Alberta.

I am a professional film and television editor based in Toronto, Canada. I discovered my passion for filmmaking as a young kid making action movies in my backyard. Later in life I discovered my favourite role, editing. I have since gained important edit room experience working on HBO’s Big Little Lies, The Art of More and Pillars of the Earth.

A resident of the Canadian Film Centre Cineplex Entertainment Film Editors Lab, I previously completed a BFA in Media Arts and Digital Technology at the Alberta University of the Arts, Calgary, Canada and completed a Graduate Certificate in Advanced Television and Film at Sheridan College, Oakville, Canada.

As a freelance editor I have had the opportunity to work in several genres including documentaries, short films, comedy, scripted narrative television and factual television. I have collaborated on many projects with great directors and show runners telling compelling stories.

My editing work has been screened at dozens of film festivals internationally, Including TIFF, FIFA Montreal, DOC LA & has been broadcast on the Discovery Channel, Cooking Channel, History Channel, Netflix, SyFy, CBC, Vice and Sony entertainment.

I’m a big believer that you are only limited by your imagination, if you can think of it you can make it.
